She has a selection of cookie cutters of different shapes and sizes.  She thinks it's unfair that some shapes are larger than others - because even if everyone gets the same number of cookies, some will end up with more dough!</p>

<p>She comes up with a plan to enlarge (or shrink) the cookie cutters' shapes --- while retaining their proportions --- so that all cookies will have the same weight.  </p>

<p>Your task in this problem is to help her draw the plans the new cookie cutters will be based on!</p>
